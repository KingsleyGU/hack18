About

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people of diverse backgrounds come together to creatively solve problems. Participants form groups in which they develop and prototype innovative ideas in a specific context (here Nordea).

Let's hack collaboration together!

Are you an employee within Trading Technology? Moreover, do you have great ideas or would you like to support your colleagues in brining their ideas to life? Then #HACK18 is for you!

Why should I join?

The #HACK18 is an event where you have the opportunity to work on an idea that really excites you! #HACK18 is 2 days of great fun and engaged colleagues working towards innovative solutions on improving processes within Nordea. During those days, you and your colleagues will work intensively on a proof of concept for your idea. A jury, consisting of members of Nordea's senior management, will eventually assess each team's solution and evaluate the idea's potential to be implemented.

The winning team takes home the #HACK18 statuette, the honour, and potentially also funding to proceed with the idea!

Do you already have a fantastic idea?

DRAFT PARAGRAPH This year ideas will be presented before the actual #HACK18 event. If you have a great idea, please describe it in the Nordea Idea Portal (LINK) and use the hashtag #HACK18. Approximately two weeks before the event, everyone that has signed up for the #HACK18 event will be invited to an idea-sharing session where all ideas from the #HACK18 marked ideas in the Idea Portal will be presented. This makes it possible for all of you participants to start to form teams, begin to look into what cool technology you could use to find the best solution for an idea, or to just to be inspired and see what all other teams are going to work on. It is not necessary for all participants to provide ideas, and several teams could work on solving the same issue.

There are essentially no limits to what you can work on, as long as you follow these three guidelines:

  • Work on something that will add value to Nordea.
  • Dedicate yourself 100% to collaborate with your team. Focus on the task at hand!
  • At the end of the second day, you demonstrate what your team has come up with.

Who can join?

#HACK18 is for everyone working within Trading Technolgy in Denmark, Sweden, Finland, Norway or Poland.

Is your daily role a developer, a project manager, an analyst, an architect, a subject matter expert or maybe an agile coach? Do you want to network, meet colleagues from other teams and solve problems together? Do you want to develop yourself and maybe try a role you are usually not working in? At #HACK18 we need the structured skills from our project managers, the analytical skills from our analysts, and the coding skills from out developers.

Everybody in Trading Technology is invited. We need all your skills and brains to craft the coolest ideas. Let's hack collaboration together!
